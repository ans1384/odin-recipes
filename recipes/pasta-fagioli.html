Pasta e Fagioli

A hearty soup with fresh vegetables, beans, and al-dente pasta.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 medium carrot, finely chopped
  • 15 ounce can cannellini beans, not drained
  • 15 ounce can tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes
  • 1 large bay leaf
  • 3 tablespoons of Italian herbs
  • 2 14 ounce cans chicken or vegetable broth
  • Salt and freshly ground pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a large heavy pot or Dutch oven, saut√© the onion and garlic in olive oil over medium heat.
  2. Blend the beans with one cup water in electric blender until almost smooth.
  3. Add blended beans to pan then add the can of tomato sauce, celery, carrots, chicken broth, Italian herbs, bay leaf, salt and pepper. Add one cup of water.
  4. Add pasta and cook uncovered until pasta is al dente, according to pasta directions. Spoon into bowls and serve with crispy bread. Enjoy!
